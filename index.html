About the Press

The Knotty Pine Press is husband-and-wife printing team Wes Lindamood and Laura Stein Lindamood, in Washington, DC. We create all our designs and print on a Kelsey 6×10. We’re the proud owners of a basement full of lead type, and design photopolymer graphics too.

The Knotty Pine Press is named for our basement, where the press is located, surrounded by mint-condition knotty pine paneling. (It is also the home of the Naughty Pine bar.)
